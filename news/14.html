Bradley Cooper looks the picture of happiness with girlfriend Irina Shayk and their daughter Lea... as they dispel rumours of strife following Lady Gaga cheating allegations

By CHARLOTTE DEAN FOR MAILONLINE

They began dating in 2015 and welcomed their daughter, Lea, two years later.

And Bradley Cooper, 44, and his girlfriend Irina Shayk, 33, looked the picture of happiness as they enjoyed a day out with their little girl on Thursday afternoon.

The couple have been plagued by rumours that Bradley was having a tryst with his A Star Is Born co-star Lady Gaga, following their entrancing duet at the Academy Awards last month.

However they quashed rumours of any strife between them, by sporting beaming smiles as they sat in the sunshine during a glorious day in LA.

Irina cut a relaxed figure in a loose-fitting white sweatshirt and swept her brunette locks up into a neat bun, while sporting a simple chain around her neck.

The model showcased her natural beauty by going make-up free for their outing, proudly showcasing her glowing complexion as she cosied up to her family.

Bradley also opted for a casual look, sporting a denim blue shirt with a pair of black slim fit jeansm complete with a pair of black and white trainers.

He sported a spattering of signature stubble and wore his hair in an care-free fashion, as he was pictured looking down adoringly at his daughter Lea, one.

Last month Lady Gaga dispelled claims she was having an affair with Bradley after their steamy Oscar performance of their tune Shallow ignited speculation.
